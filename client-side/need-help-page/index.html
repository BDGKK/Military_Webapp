Registration

If you alredy have an account please log in to the web page by entering your email and user password. If you don't have an account please go to the registraion tab in the home page and upload your details.

If your details are confirmed by the admins, you will have your own profile in this web page. To see your own profile please click on the profile tab in our services. Registration Tab


Forget password

If you forget your password please go through this link and create a new password.Forget password tab


Pension

After registering to the our military salute web page If you want to apply for the pension, please click on the download application tab >> pension tab to go to the pension application page. If you can't find pension application page please go through this link. Pension Application tab

your monthly pension will calculate as a precentage of the salary you had when you leave from the service.

If you are eligible for pension, The date of you recieve your monthly payment will inform you through your user email address.

you can take a direct look at your pension summery by cliking Pension tab.


Loan

After registering to the our military salute web page If you want to apply for the loan, please click on the download application tab >> loan tab to go to the loan application page. If you can't find loan application page please go through this link. Loan Application Tab

your monthly interset payment will be calculate based on the interest rate for time period you selected in your loan application

If you are eligible for recieve the loan, The date of you have to pay your monthly payment will inform you through your user email address.

Submission of your documents and your parton documents to confirm your loan application is mandatory.

you can take a direct look at your loan summery by cliking Loan tab.
